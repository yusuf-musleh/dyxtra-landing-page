features



Real Time Tracking

We track based on the location of the driver's phone, display the location of your customer's food/ride/package on a map while updating and moving in real-time.

No App? No Problem

Think of us as Tracking-as-a-Service, whether you have an existing App you can easily integrate our tracking to it, or you can download our App and use it instead. No coding at all.

Effortless Integration & Use

Your business does not need expensive hardware or man power. Your customers do not need to install anything, they track their items on their phone, tablet or computer.
